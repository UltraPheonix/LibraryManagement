Detailed Description

Manages a collection of books within the library's system.

The Inventory class handles the storage, retrieval, and management of books in a library. It supports operations such as adding, removing, searching for books by title or ISBN, checking availability, listing available or checked-out books, and updating book statuses. The inventory uses hashing to store and locate books efficiently.
